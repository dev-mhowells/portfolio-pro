Recent Work

I’m currently working on expanding the projects the projects below in order to bring them to a real user base!

Language correction messenger

Allows teachers to correct messages from students in real time. Students can also easily call attention to words they don’t know. Teachers can then provide synonyms, examples and pronunciation for these words through text and audio.

Made with: HTML, CSS, Tailwind, JavaScript, React and Firebase.

English Deck

A a minimum viable product for an online-learning platform based around articles with built in flashcards, a quiz, and comment section. Flashcards can be flipped, cycled through and saved for review. A quiz at the end checks article comprehension. Readers can log in and practice using key words in the comment section, which checks off these words as they are typed by the user. The app is also hooked up to a custom built content management system.

Made with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Firebase.

English Deck CMS

A custom-built content management system for my English Deck online learning app. The CMS allows me to update the database from which English Deck draws its data for all elements of the site, from the landing page to the individual elements of its interactive articles.

Made with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Firebase.
