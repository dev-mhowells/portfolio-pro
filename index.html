Recent Work

I’m currently working on expanding the projects I’ve been working on in order to bring them to a real user base.

Messenger for language learners

Allows teachers to correct messages from students. Students can also easily call attention to words they don’t know. Teachers can then provide synonyms, examples and pronunciation for these words through text and audio.

Made with: HTML, CSS, Tailwind, JavaScript, React and Firebase.

English Deck

An article with built in flashcards, a quiz, and comment section. Flashcards can be flipped, cycled through and saved for review. A quiz at the end checks article comprehension. Readers can log in and practice using key words in the comment section, which checks off these words as they are typed by the user.

Made with: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React and Firebase.
